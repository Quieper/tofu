geothermal electric plants were traditionally built exclusively on the edges of tectonic plates where high temperature geothermal resources are available near the surface . the development of binary cycle power plants and improvements in drilling and extraction technology enable enhanced geothermal systems over a much greater geographical range . [ 32 ] demonstration projects are operational in landau - pfalz , germany , and soultz - sous - forets , france , while an earlier effort in basel , switzerland was shut down after it triggered earthquakes . other demonstration projects are under construction in australia , the united kingdom , and the united states of america .
