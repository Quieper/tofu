hot springs have been used for bathing at least since paleolithic times . [ 12 ] the oldest known spa is a stone pool on china lisan mountain built in the qin dynasty in the 3rd century bc , at the same site where the huaqing chi palace was later built . in the first century ad , romans conquered aquae sulis , now bath , somerset , england , and used the hot springs there to feed public baths and underfloor heating . the admission fees for these baths probably represent the first commercial use of geothermal power . the world oldest geothermal district heating system in chaudes - aigues , france , has been operating since the 15th century . [ 13 ] the earliest industrial exploitation began in 1827 with the use of geyser steam to extract boric acid from volcanic mud in larderello , italy .
