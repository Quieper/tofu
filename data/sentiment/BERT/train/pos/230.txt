a few years back , google attempted to purchase impossible foods , a company founded by stanford biochemist pat brown , who is the founder of the open - access peer - reviewed public library of science ( plos ) journals , reportedly for close to $ 300 million . brown and his team are looking at meat at a molecular level and attempting to replicate all aspects of it , using innovative food technologies . impossible foods has developed a plant - based product that looks and tastes like ground beef . when my team and i had a private tasting some months before the official launch at david chang momofuku , one of our scientists who is a long - time vegetarian found it so meat - like that she could not eat it .
