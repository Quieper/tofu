according to doe , an average of 25 billion barrels of hot water is produced in united states oil and gas wells each year . this water , which has historically been viewed as an inconvenience to well operators , could be harnessed to produce up to 3 gigawatts of clean , reliable baseload energy [ 16 ] . this energy could not only reduce greenhouse gas emissions , it could also increase profitability and extend the economic life of existing oil and gas field infrastructure . the doe geothermal technologies office is working toward a goal of achieving widespread production of low - temperature geothermal power by 2020 .
