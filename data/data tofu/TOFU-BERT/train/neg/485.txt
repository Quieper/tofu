wind turbines are known to pose a threat to the wildlife . flying birds and bats whose habitats or migratory paths could be injured or killed if they run into the blades that turn on the fanlike structure of wind turbines when they are spinning . the deaths of birds and bats are a controversial subject at wind farm sites , which has raised concerns by fish and wildlife conservation groups . [ 5 ] aside from the wildlife that flies through the air , wildlife on the ground may also affected by the noise pollution generated from whirring blades . although wind turbines can cause problems for wildlife , other things such as skyscraper and large windows are also hazardous and continue to be built without question or similar outcry .
