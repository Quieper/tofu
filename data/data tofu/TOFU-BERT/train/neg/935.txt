most diseases that vaccines target are relatively harmless in many cases , thus making vaccines unnecessary . the chickenpox is often just a rash with blisters and can be treated with acetaminophen , cool compresses , and calamine lotion . [ 106 ] the measles is normally a rash accompanied by a fever and runny nose and can be treated with rest and fluids . [ 107 ] [ 108 ] rubella is often just a virus with a rash and low fever and can be treated with acetaminophen . [ 72 ] [ 109 ] rotavirus can normally be treated with hydration and probiotics .
