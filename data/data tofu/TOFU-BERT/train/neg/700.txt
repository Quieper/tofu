the department of interior canceled the crescent peak wind project in southern nevada next to castle mountains national monument and south mccullough wilderness area , the bureau of land management ( blm ) confirms to basin and range watch . thanks to a large local grassroots push to halt this ill - sited wind project and protect the rare desert grasslands and biodiverse joshua tree woodlands here . interior cited recreational values of this area , mining and hunting , as well as the blm cancellation of its southern nevada resource management plan . historic resources , tribal concerns and archaeology were also concerns .
