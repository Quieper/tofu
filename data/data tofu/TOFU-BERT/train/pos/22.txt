in 2013 , mark post , professor at maastricht university , presented the first hamburger made of cultured meat . since then , the dream of being able to create and consume meat based on " cellular agriculture " , without livestock farming , has gained support among both animal - rights activists and , especially , players in the industry . a large number of start - ups have been created , with many sponsored by big names in the food industry . the ambition is to market cultured beef , poultry or fish meat at an affordable price by 2020 or 202 .
