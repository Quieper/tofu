vaccines save children and their parents time and money . vaccines cost less in time and money to obtain than infectious diseases cost in time off of work to care for a sick child , potential long - term disability care , and medical costs . [ 67 ] for example , children under five with the flu are contagious for about eight days , and , according to a 2012 cdc study , cost their parents an average of 11 to 73 hours of wages ( about $ 222 to $ 1 , 456 ) and $ 300 to $ 4 , 000 in medical expenses . [ 68 ] [ 69 ] children with rotavirus are contagious for up to 30 days . [ 70 ] a 2018 study found that each case of measles in arkansas cost the health department $ 47 , 962 . [ 125 ] as of may 20 , there were 880 cases of measles in 24 states in 2019 , costing taxpayers an estimated $ 42 . 2 million . [ 125 ] [ 126 ] furthermore , under the patient protection and affordable care act ( ppaca , or obamacare ) many vaccines are available to children and adults without copay . [ 71 ]
