in 2016 , the majority of energy added to the us power grid was made up of renewable technologies , with thousands of jobs and high growth associated with such emerging industries . every indication is that this pattern will continue , with 93 % of the total utility - scale generating capacity added to the grid accounted for by solar , wind , and natural gas . this puts the us up to 4th in solar pv usage , still lagging behind china , germany , and japan , and shows that solar is the way of the future .
