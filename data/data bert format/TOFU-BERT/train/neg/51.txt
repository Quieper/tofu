detox tea programs usually come in two parts : a daytime tea , and a nighttime one . the daytime tea is riddled with caffeine from blend of yerba mate , guarana , and green tea . caffeine is known to have a diuretic effect . while some studies have shown a link between caffeine consumption and weight loss , others have also gone on to find a link between caffeine consumption and weight gain , leaving the evidence for weight loss from caffeine consumption in the dark . but daytime teas do not stop at caffeine : some also contain dandelion root , another known diuretic . " the weight loss [ from detox teas ] is primarily , and probably all , water weight , " dr . karin kratina , a nationally recognized nutrition therapist , tells teen vogue . " if true weight loss occurs , it is because a caloric deficit also occurred from a change in eating habits . "
