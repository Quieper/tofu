offshore wind development offers enormous potential to help meet california 100 % clean electricity target by 2045 . california offshore wind resource has the technical potential to produce 392 twh per year , about 150 % of california annual electricity load . the offshore wind resource near humboldt bay is among the best in the nation , with wind speeds often exceeding 10 meters per second at 90 meters above the ocean surface - and even faster at heights around 120 meters , where the turbines are likely to be centered . furthermore , offshore wind speeds have a consistent profile between different seasons and throughout the day ( with a peak in the early evening ) , offering the ability to provide stable power throughout the year and help balance the grid as solar power goes offline at dusk .
