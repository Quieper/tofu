several researchers already are using crspr to edit genes . gene editing research in animal models involving mice and monkeys , in human embryo , and in hiv - infected human cells show promise . for example , in 2016 lu you , md , and colleagues at sichuan university in chengdu became the first group in the world to inject crispr - cas9 modified t cells into patients with non - small cell lung cancer . the technology also is being used to speed pharmaceutical research and identify targeted therapies for somatic and heritable diseases .
