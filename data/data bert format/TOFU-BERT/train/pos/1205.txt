geothermal energy is clean , sustainable , environment friendly , cost - effective and reliable . geothermal energy resources range from the shallow ground to hot water and hot rock found a few miles beneath the earth surface . it can also be found deeper into the earth interior due to the extremely high temperatures and pressures of molten rock called magma . in the united states , most geothermal reservoirs of hot water are located in the western states , alaska , and hawaii .
